ABOUT


Sam Vancayseele (°1994) is a student in Graphics- and Drawingarts at LUCA School Of Arts Ghent. His work consists mainly of realistic charcoal drawings. Until 2015, he was mainly inspired by the landscape and nature. These days he goes in search for forms of banality . Words or images he encountersor appaer in his thoughts, have a chance to be formed on paper.  Just like him his work is white noise between serious and trivial .
